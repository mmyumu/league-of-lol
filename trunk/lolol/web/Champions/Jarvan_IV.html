          Jarvan Lightshield IV, the Exemplar of Demacia is a champion in League of Legends.[1]

          Abilities

          Martial Cadence

            Jarvan IV's first attack on a target deals 6% / 8% / 10% of their current health as bonus physical damage. This caps at 400 damage. This effect cannot occur on the same target more than once every 6 seconds.

            Ability Details
            Martial Cadence is an on-hit effect ability.
            • The triggering autoattack will apply other on-hit effects and can critical strike as normal.
              • Unlike most other on-hit physical damage, Martial Cadence's bonus damage does not apply life steal. This is a special case, perhaps to prevent abuse or for flavor. The triggering attack will still apply life steal.
              • Martial Cadence's bonus damage will affect structures.
              • Martial Cadence will deal 0.5 points of bonus damage to wards.
              • Critical strikes do not interact with Martial Cadence's bonus damage.
              • Martial Cadence not be applied if the attack is  parried,  dodged,  blocked, or Jarvan is blinded.
            • Spell shields will not block the on-hit effect.


            Dragon Strike
            • Range: 770
            • Cooldown: 10 / 9 / 8 / 7 / 6
            • Cost: 45 / 50 / 55 / 60 / 65 mana

            Active: Jarvan IV extends his lance in a straight line, dealing physical damage and lowering the armor of all enemies hit for 3 seconds. Additionally, if it encounters his Demacian Standard, it will pull Jarvan to it, knocking up enemies within 160 range along his path for 0.75 seconds.

            • Physical Damage: 70 / 115 / 160 / 205 / 250 (+ 120% bonus AD)
            • Armor Reduction: 10 / 14 / 18 / 22 / 26%
            Ability Details
            Dragon Strike is a pass-through linear skillshot.
            • Applies spell effects as an area of effect ability.
              • Spell vamp is reduced to one-third effectiveness.
              • Rylai's Crystal Scepter will apply a 15% slow.
            • Spell shields will block the ability.

            Additional Information:

            • Dragon Strike will still pull Jarvan to his  Demacian Standard if he is immobilized, though it will not remove the immobilize itself.


            Golden Aegis
            • Range: 300
            • Cooldown: 20 / 18 / 16 / 14 / 12
            • Cost: 45 / 50 / 55 / 60 / 65 mana

            Active: Jarvan IV slows enemies within range for 2 seconds and shields himself for up to 5 seconds. The shield gains strength for each enemy champion within range upon activation.

            • Shield Strength: 50 / 90 / 130 / 170 / 210 (+ 20 / 30 / 40 / 50 / 60 per nearby enemy champion)
            • Max Shield Strength: 150 / 240 / 330 / 420 / 510
            • Slow: 15 / 20 / 25 / 30 / 35%
            Ability Details
            Golden Aegis is a point-blank area of effect slow and a self-targeted absorption shield.
            • Spell shields will block the slow.

            Additional Information:

            • Golden Aegis has no cast time and will not interrupt Jarvan's previous orders.


            Demacian Standard
            • Range: 830
            • Cooldown: 13
            • Cost: 55 mana

            Passive: Permanently grants Jarvan IV bonus armor and attack speed.

            • Armor: 10 / 13 / 16 / 19 / 22
            • Attack Speed: 10 / 13 / 16 / 19 / 22%

            Active: Jarvan IV throws a Demacian flag to the target location, dealing magic damage to enemies within 75 range. The flag will remain for 8 seconds, providing vision in a radius of 700 and an aura that increases the attack speed of allies within a radius of 600.

            • Magic Damage: 60 / 105 / 150 / 195 / 240 (+ 80% AP)
            • Attack Speed Aura: 10 / 13 / 16 / 19 / 22%
            Ability Details
            Demacian Standard is a ground-targeted area of effect.
            • Applies spell effects as an area of effect ability.
              • Spell vamp is reduced to one-third effectiveness.
              • Rylai's Crystal Scepter will apply a 15% slow.
            • Magic damage will be mitigated by  Black Shield and Lifeline.
            • Spell shields will block the damage.

            Additional Information:

            • The Standard is considered a unit and can be targeted by ally-targetable abilities such as Teleport and  Lee Sin's  Safeguard.


            Cataclysm
            • Range: 650
            • Cooldown: 120 / 105 / 90
            • Cost: 100 / 125 / 150 mana

            Active: Jarvan IV leaps to an enemy champion, dealing physical damage to it and creating a 325-radius circle of impassable terrain around themselves. This circle lasts for 3.5 seconds and also grants sight within 825 range. Jarvan IV can prematurely destroy the impassable terrain by activating this ability again.

            • Physical Damage: 200 / 325 / 450 (+ 150% bonus AD)
            Ability Details
            Cataclysm is a targeted area of effect dash that creates an impassable wall around Jarvan when he lands.
            • Applies spell effects as an area of effect ability.
              • Spell vamp is reduced to one-third effectiveness.
              • Rylai's Crystal Scepter will apply a 15% slow.
            • Spell shields will block the damage.

            Additional Information:

            • Cataclysm does not track targets who change their location such as with dash abilities or Flash.
            • Cataclysm's ring is not considered terrain for the purposes of abilities that interact with it such as  Vayne's  Condemn and  Poppy's  Heroic Charge.
